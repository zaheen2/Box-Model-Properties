About Google

Google is a multinational technology company known for its search engine, which is the most widely used in the world. Founded in 1998 by Larry Page and Sergey Brin, Google offers various products and services, including Gmail, Google Maps, Google Drive, and the Android operating system. The company is a leader in digital advertising, cloud computing, and artificial intelligence. Its mission is to organize the world's information and make it universally accessible and useful, while constantly innovating to enhance user experience and technology.
